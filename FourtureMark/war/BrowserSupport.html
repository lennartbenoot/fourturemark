Browser Support

Browser support is a priority for us. We try to be compatible with the majority and most popular browsers.

Nevertheless, browsers evolve extremely fast currently and it does take time before we can ensure full compatibility. Therefore we prefer to inform our users proactively on not yet compatible browsers and provide alternatives.
